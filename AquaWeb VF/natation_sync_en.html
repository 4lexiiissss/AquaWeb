Synchronized swimming

Synchronized swimming, also known as artistic swimming, is an aquatic sport that combines swimming, dancing and water gymnastics. This discipline requires great physical strength coordination and mastery of technique to execute graceful and complex movements movements while remaining perfectly synchronized with the music.
